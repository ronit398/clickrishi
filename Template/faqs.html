QUESTION NO. 1

What is the problem faced by the farmers ?

Farmers are not being able to understand what amount of crops are to be sent to which cities so that they can gain maximum profit. The study and research conducted on the Supply chain of Fruits and Vegetables in India suggest that there is an improper supply chain management which leads to maximum inefficiencies and resulting to losses and wastage of Fruits and Vegetables and taking farmer’s lives.

QUESTION NO. 2

How does this application solves farmer's problem ?

This application empowers farmers to become entrepreneurs by deciding to which city they should sell their crops so as to generate maximum profit by reducing the role of middleman from the agricultural industry also regulating the quantity of seeds they plan to buy which will help control their losses.
